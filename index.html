The future of MARKET STREET is in YOUR HANDS

As part of the City’s official redesign process for Market, the Market Street Prototyping Festival will use a hands-on approach to inspire a shared vision for the street.

For three days, over a mile of San Francisco’s main thoroughfare will showcase new ideas to improve lives by improving our city’s public space.
Up to 50 projects will be chosen from hundreds of submissions from San Francisco and beyond.
Over the course of the Festival, the projects will be seen over 500,000 times from foot traffic alone.
